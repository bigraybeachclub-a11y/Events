Big Ray's Grand Opening Tour About

The Grand Opening Tour is a celebration of excellence, marking the official debut of our world-class golf destination. Set amidst 200 acres of breathtaking scenery, this exclusive event invites you to experience the very best in golf, hospitality, and entertainment.

Enjoy our meticulously designed 18-hole championship course, state-of-the-art clubhouse, and exceptional dining—all crafted to deliver an unforgettable experience for every guest. The Grand Opening Tour is more than an event; it's the beginning of a new tradition in luxury golf.

Be part of history as we open our doors and welcome you to a weekend filled with excitement, camaraderie, and the spirit of the game. Reserve your place at this landmark occasion and discover what makes our course truly extraordinary.
