Who Needs a Visa for Ghana?

  • Most travelers need a visa to enter Ghana. Some ECOWAS and select African countries are exempt.
  • Check the official list of visa-exempt countries.

Types of Visas

  • Tourist Visa
  • Business Visa
  • Transit Visa
  • Student/Work/Other Visas

How to Apply

  • Apply at the nearest Ghanaian embassy or consulate.
  • Submit a completed application form, passport photos, and supporting documents.
  • Pay the visa fee (varies by type and nationality).
  • Processing time: 3-10 business days (varies by location).

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photos
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Return/onward ticket
  • Proof of funds
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate

Visa on Arrival

  • Available for select nationalities with prior approval. Learn more.

Tips for a Smooth Application

  • Apply early to avoid delays.
  • Double-check all documents before submission.
  • Carry copies of your visa and important documents when traveling.
